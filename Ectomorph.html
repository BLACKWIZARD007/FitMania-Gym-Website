Ectomorph Diet Plan

Meal 1 - Breakfast

  • 1 scoop whey isolate
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 banana
  • 1/4 cup oats
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • Cocoa/chocolate syrup

Totals:

  • 630–660 Calories
  • 40g Protein
  • 60g Carbs
  • 25g Fat

Meal 2 - Snack

  • 2 whole-wheat bread slices
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp jam
  • 1 banana

Totals:

  • 500–530 Calories
  • 15g Protein
  • 80–90g Carbs
  • 15g Fat

Meal 3 – Lunch

  • 150g chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup brown rice
  • Green salad
  • 1 tbsp butter

Totals:

  • 470–500 Calories
  • 40g Protein
  • 75g Carbs
  • 6g Fat

Meal 4 – Pre-Workout Shake

  • 1 scoop whey protein
  • 1 scoop creatine
  • 1 cup fat-free milk
  • 1 banana

Totals:

  • 350 Calories
  • 26g Protein
  • 60g Carbs
  • 2g Fat

Meal 5 – Post-Workout Shake

  • 1 scoop whey protein
  • BCAA serving
  • 1 scoop dextrose
  • 1 cup fat-free milk
  • Grapes
  • 20g dark chocolate

Totals:

  • 560–600 Calories
  • 27g Protein
  • 100–120g Carbs
  • 8g Fat

Meal 6 – Dinner

  • 150g chicken breast or tuna
  • 1/2 cup brown rice
  • Green salad
  • 1 tbsp butter

Totals:

  • 450–480 Calories
  • 40g Protein
  • 60g Carbs
  • 10g Fat
